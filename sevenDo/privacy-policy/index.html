Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

SevenDo ("the App") is developed and operated by Stylish Engineering. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using SevenDo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google OAuth, we collect the following from your authentication provider:

  • Display name (your Google account name)
  • Email address
  • Avatar URL (your Google profile picture URL)
  • Login provider (e.g., "google")

We do not collect passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by your OAuth provider through Supabase Auth.

1.2 User-Generated Content

  • Tasks — title, optional description, scheduled date, completion status, and ordering position.
  • Messages — if you use the in-app feedback chat to communicate with us, the message content is stored.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, or files from your device.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage telemetry beyond what Supabase infrastructure logs at the server level.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and operate the App:

  • Account information — to authenticate you and display your profile within the App.
  • Tasks — to store, sync, and display your to-do items across your devices.
  • Messages — to enable feedback communication between you and the app administrator.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the App's core functionality.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored in Supabase, hosted in the EU (Stockholm, Sweden).
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption in transit).
  • Data at rest is stored in Supabase's managed PostgreSQL database. We do not apply additional encryption at the application level beyond what Supabase provides by default.
  • Access to your data is enforced through Row Level Security (RLS) policies — each user can only access their own tasks, profile, and messages.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your data may only be disclosed if required by law or legal process (e.g., a valid court order).

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data is retained for as long as your account exists.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data from the Settings page within the App.
  • Upon account deletion, your profile, tasks, messages, and any related data are permanently removed from our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

SevenDo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data (available via Settings).
  • Data portability — request your data in a portable format.
  • Restriction / Objection — request restriction of or object to certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Contract performance — to provide the service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interest — to maintain and improve the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify users through the App or by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
